Tofayel Ahmed – Senior Web Developer, SEO Expert & Entrepreneur

Tofayel Ahmed is a seasoned Senior Web Developer with over seven years of experience, specializing in e-commerce solutions. He has collaborated with companies like AR Fashion & Graphics Ltd. and drybazaar.com, mastering technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MERN stack, PHP, and Python.

As an SEO expert, Tofayel leads a dedicated team that ensures websites rank at the top of search engines while maintaining robust WordPress security. He also designs engaging WordPress websites, turning clients' visions into reality.
